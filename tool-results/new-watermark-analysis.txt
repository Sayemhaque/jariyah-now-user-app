PRACTICAL IMPACT FOR VIDEO WATERMARK USE:
  - If composited over video as-is, this image will lay a faint green tint
    over the ENTIRE frame (because alpha is non-zero almost everywhere),
    not just behind the white logo.
  - That is almost certainly NOT what you want for a watermark. You
    typically want only the white logo strokes visible, with the rest
    fully transparent (alpha = 0).
  - Recommended fix before using as a watermark:

         logo remains; OR

         on alpha=0 background, no green halo); OR

         dominate the video frame.
